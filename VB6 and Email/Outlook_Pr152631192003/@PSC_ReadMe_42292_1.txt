Title: [A1 Outlook Professional - Address Book Backup and Restore
Description: Backup and Restore your Outlook Address Books contacts. You can save your contacts as different profiles which means you can maintain different contact books and restore it whenever and wherever you want! Saves you considerable of time and resources since your address book is now intact and is stored in a Central repository. Ideal for a network environment with a central Database server(SQL Server, preferably).
